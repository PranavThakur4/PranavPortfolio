ABOUT ME

Intro

Thakur Pranav Gopal Singh is a passionate and innovative engineer currently pursuing a Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering & Material Science, Aerospace Specialization at Duke University. He holds a Bachelor of Technology in Mechanical Engineering, Manufacturing Minors from Manipal Institute of Technology.

His professional experience includes serving as Production & Operations Manager at Balaji CNC Technologies, where he specialized in CNC operations, CAD/CAM, quality control, and implemented AS9100 D certification processes. He also completed an internship at the Defense Research & Development Laboratory (DRDL, DRDO), focusing on pressure regulator modeling and micro-thruster research.

Pranav is a prolific project leader, notably with ThrustMIT, a student rocketry team, where he led launch operations and played a key role in designing sounding rockets and payloads for the Spaceport America Cup. His projects also include designing control systems for aircraft pitch and analyzing heat exchangers. He has a patent filed for a novel non-pyrotechnic radial deployment mechanism for payloads in sounding rockets and has published research papers.

He possesses a strong skill set in CAD/CAM software (Mastercam, CATIA, Fusion, SolidWorks), simulation tools (ANSYS, MATLAB, AMESim), and programming languages (Python, HTML, CSS, SQL). His accomplishments include high rankings in the Spaceport America Cup 2023 & 2024.
